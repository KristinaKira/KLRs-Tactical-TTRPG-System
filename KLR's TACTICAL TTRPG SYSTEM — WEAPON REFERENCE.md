## **WEAPON STATS KEY:**

- **DMG:** Damage — dice rolled when hit connects
- **AC:** Action Cost — actions spent to use this weapon
- **AMO:** Ammo Type — what this weapon consumes
- **SPC:** Special Ability — triggered on LCK roll (D20) after damage
- **RNG:** Range — max distance in spaces
- **DT:** Damage Type — what it damages (Armor / Shield / Both)
- **W:** Weight — carry weight units
- **VAL:** Value — cost in currency

## **DAMAGE TYPES & AMMO TYPES**

**DAMAGE TYPES:**

- **Explosive:** Damages Armor & Shields
- **Ballistic:** Damages Armor
- **Physical:** Damages Armor
- **Energy:** Damages Shields only
- **Elemental:** Damages Armor & Shields

**AMMO TYPES:**

- **Ballistic:** All ballistic/conventional guns
- **Energy:** All energy-based guns
- **Magic:** All mages & magic users
- **Explosives:** All explosive weapons
- **Consumables:** Throwable / single-use items

---

## **BASIC WEAPON FLOW:**

**Step 1:** Roll D20 to hit target  
**Step 2:** Roll DMG dice if hit connects  
**Step 3:** Roll D20 for SPC ability (LCK dependent, after damage)

**Note:** Guns require a reload action after each turn of use
