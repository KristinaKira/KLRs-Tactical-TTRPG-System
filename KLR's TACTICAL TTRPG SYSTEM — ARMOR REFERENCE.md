## **ARMOR STATS KEY:**

- **PROTECTION:** Armor points (absorbs damage before HP)
- **WEIGHT:** Carry weight units (affects action slots)
- **DAMAGE TYPE:** What this armor protects against
- **VALUE:** Cost in currency
- **NOTES:** Special properties or requirements

---

## **ARMOR DEGRADATION:**

**At end of encounter, roll D4:**

- **Roll 1:** Armor degrades (loses 2 protection)
- **Roll 2-4:** Armor holds

**Example:**

- Combat Armor starts at 12 protection
- After degradation: drops to 10 protection
- Needs repair to restore

**Repair costs:** Varies by armor tier (DM discretion or % of value)

---

## **DAMAGE TYPE INTERACTIONS:**

**From Damage Types table:**

|Damage Type|What It Hurts|
|---|---|
|**Physical**|Armor only|
|**Ballistic**|Armor only|
|**Explosive**|Armor & Shields|
|**Energy**|Shields only|
|**Elemental**|Armor & Shields|

**So:**

- **Light Kevlar** (Ballistic protection) = good vs guns, bad vs swords
- **Energy Shield Vest** = good vs lasers, bad vs bullets
- **Heavy Combat Suit** (Physical, Ballistic, Explosive) = versatile tank armor
